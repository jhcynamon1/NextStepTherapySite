Hi, I'm Jesse

I believe that seeking therapy is one of the most courageous things a person can do. It takes strength to acknowledge when we need support and vulnerability to trust someone with our struggles.

My approach is straightforward and collaborative. I won't pretend to have all the answers, but I will walk alongside you as we figure things out together. I draw from evidence-based approaches like Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) and mindfulness practices, but ultimately, the relationship we build is what creates the space for healing.

I understand the unique pressures people face — whether that's the weight of societal expectations, the challenge of major life changes, or simply feeling lost in your own life. Whatever brought you here, you don't have to navigate it alone.
